About us

Fabric Terminal is the first ever B2B mobile application that connects the fabric suppliers & garment manufacturers across the country. It is a one-stop sourcing platform where suppliers & manufacturers can directly communicate, exchange information about the rate, availability of fabric & get detailed statistics of all the activities performed. The app resolves all the hurdles in the textile industry. It provides the suppliers and manufacturers with insights into real active trends & brings them the power of technology to develop their business without being a mediator.
